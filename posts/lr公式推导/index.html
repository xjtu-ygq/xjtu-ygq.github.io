LR公式推导

2023.02.02

线性回归公式： $$ y=w^Tx+b $$ 简化线性回归公式： $$ y=w^Tx $$ sigmoid公式： $$ \sigma(x)=\frac{1}{1+e^{-x}} $$ LR公式： $$ \pi(x)=\frac{1}{1+e^{-w^Tx}} $$ 二分类： $$ P(y=1|x)=\frac{1}{1+e^{-w^Tx}} $$

$$ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} P(y=0|x)&=1-P(y=1|x)\ &=\frac{e^{-w^Tx}}{1+e^{-w^Tx}} \end{aligned} \end{equation} $$

对数似然函数： $$ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} L(x)&= \prod\limits^N_{i=1}logP(y_i|x_i)\\ &=\sum\limits^N_{i=1}(y_ilog(p_1)+(1-y_ilog(p_0)))\\ &=\sum\limits^N_{i=1}(y_ilog(p_1)+(1-y_ilog(1-p_1)))\\ &=\sum\limits^N_{i=1}(y_i(log(p_1)-log(1-p_1))+log(1-p_1))\\ &=\sum\limits^N_{i=1}(y_ilog\frac{p_1}{1-p_1}+log(1-p_1))\\ &=\sum\limits^N_{i=1}(y_iw^Tx_i-log(1+e^{w^Tx})) \end{aligned} \end{equation} $$ 求偏导： $$ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \frac{\partial L(x)}{\partial w}&=\sum\limits^N_{i=1}(y_ix_i-\frac{x_ie^{w^Tx_i}}{1+e^{w^Tx_i}})\ &=\sum\limits^N_{i=1}(y_i-\frac{e^{w^Tx_i}}{1+e^{w^Tx_i}})x_i\ &=\sum\limits^N_{i=1}(y_i-\frac{1}{1+e^{-w^Tx_i}})x_i\ &=\sum\limits^N_{i=1}(y_i-\pi(w^Tx))x_i\ \end{aligned} \end{equation} $$ 梯度上升： $$ w_j:=w_j+\alpha\sum\limits^N_{i=1}(y_i-\pi(w^Tx))x_i $$ 参考：逻辑回归（Logistic Regression，LR）公式推导
